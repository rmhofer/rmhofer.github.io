Hi! I'm a PhD candidate in MIT's [Brain and Cognitive Sciences Department](https://bcs.mit.edu) where I work in the [Computational Psycholinguistics Laboratory](http://cpl.mit.edu). I am supervised by [Roger Levy](http://www.mit.edu/~rplevy/) and [Josh Tenenbaum](http://web.mit.edu/cocosci/josh.html).

Earlier I obtained a [MSc in Cognitive Science](https://www.meicogsci.eu) from the [University of Vienna](https://www.univie.ac.at/en/), worked at the [VisionLab](https://www.visionlab-ceu.org) at [Central European University](https://www.ceu.edu) and the [Center for Adaptive Behavior and Cognition (ABC)](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/research/concluded-areas/center-for-adaptive-behavior-and-cognition) at the [Max Planck Institute for Human Development in Berlin](https://www.mpib-berlin.mpg.de/en).

In my research I combine behavioral experiments and [computational modeling](https://probmods.org) to gain insights into how communication systems are learned and how they evolve over time.

I am one of the co-founders of the [MIT BCS Philosopy](https://sites.google.com/view/mit-bcs-philosophy/home) Circle. Besides research, I enjoy [photography]({{ "/photography" | relative_url }}) and making electronic music.
